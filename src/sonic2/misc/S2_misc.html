Good news! You don't need any external tools at all for many of the texts in Sonic 2, because the community disassembly already comes with macros for most of them.

Here's a list of all the text-related things you can edit directly in s2.asm by hand:
Text type Location in s2.asm
Credits texts byte_B55C:
"SONIC AND MILES IN" text byte_BCE9:
Options texts byte_97CA:
2P VS mode texts byte_874A:
